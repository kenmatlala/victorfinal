About

Masego Molefe, a trailblazing mental health advocate, founded Rudisa Thari Pty (Ltd) Holistic Mental Health Services in 2024. Born out of her personal experiences navigating social challenges that impacted mental wellness, Masego's mission is to revolutionize access to mental health care. She is determined to harness the transformative power of mental wellness to drive positive change. She is dedicated to empowering individuals to break free from debilitating mental patterns, achieving emotional and psychological well-being. .

  • Our Values

    "Lehumo la motho ke botho."

    Confidentiality: Providing a safe, secure space for all clients.
    Respect: Holistic support, respecting individual limits and boundaries.
    Accessibility: Striving to make mental health services available to more people.
    Professionalism: Maintaining high standards in all interactions.
    Commitment: Advocating for mental health at every opportunity.

  • Experiences

    "Isifuba esikhulu siyaqonda"

    With a unique blend of experience as a camp counselor, leader, and committed community volunteer, Masego possesses a rare ability to connect with diverse populations. Her academic journey has been marked by a relentless passion for community service, volunteering at children's and women's homes. Rudisa Thari is built on the fundamental belief that mental health care is a universal right, not a privilege. By harnessing the power of holistic mental health services, Masego aims to shatter barriers, ensuring that liberation from mental health challenges is within reach for every individual.
